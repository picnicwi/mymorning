A new study published in Nature finds that human scientists continue to surpass the best AI agents when tackling complex, multi-step scientific tasks. Databricks

Azure Databricks Launches Lakebase GA — A Database Built for AI Agents

At FabCon 2026, Azure Databricks announced the general availability of Lakebase, a managed serverless Postgres service built into the lakehouse, designed as the transactional system of record for AI agents. Databricks also launched Genie Code for agentic data engineering, a free tier for Lakeflow Connect, and new integrations with Excel and Microsoft Teams. Lakebase enables AI agents to read, write, and track state across complex multi-step workflows.

Why it matters Purpose-built transactional databases for AI agents represent the next evolution of data infrastructure, blurring the line between analytical and operational data platforms.
Data Platforms 02 / 03
Microsoft Azure

Microsoft Fabric Unifies Databases and Analytics on a Single Platform at FabCon

Microsoft used FabCon 2026 and SQLCon to unveil its vision for unifying transactional databases and analytics under the Microsoft Fabric umbrella, positioning it as a single governance and access model for all enterprise data. New offerings Foundry IQ and Fabric IQ simplify connecting disparate systems and data sources, while native reading from OneLake via Azure Databricks Unity Catalog moved to public preview. Microsoft acquired AI-driven data engineering platform Osmos to fold automated data preparation into Fabric.

Why it matters The convergence of transactional and analytical data on one platform is critical for AI applications that need both — organizations maintaining separate domains face increasing operational friction.

Microsoft and Databricks announced enhanced openness and interoperability with OneLake, even as the two companies compete for enterprise data platform dominance.
